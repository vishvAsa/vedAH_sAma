***Upaniṣad text:***

**‘When one has Faith, then he reflects; without Faith, one does not reflect; one reflects only when he has Faith.—But one should seek to understand Faith itself.’—‘I wish, Revered sir, to understand Faith.’—(1)**

**Commentary (Śaṅkara Bhāṣya):**

*Faith* is the idea that thing in question really exists.—(1)

End of Section (19) of Discourse VII.
