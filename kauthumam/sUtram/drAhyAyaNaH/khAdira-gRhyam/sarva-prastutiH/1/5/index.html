सूर्यायेति प्रातः पूर्वाम् १५

Oldenberg ...{Loading}...
  1. In the morning the first (oblation with the formula), ‘To Sūrya (Svāhā)!’

Oldenberg ...{Loading}...
  1. The wiping round the fire and the similar acts, with the exception of the sprinkling (of water) round (the fire), are omitted here.

पत्नी जुहुयादित्येके १७

Oldenberg ...{Loading}...
  1. Some (teachers say) that his wife may offer these oblations, for the wife is (as it were) the house, and that fire is the domestic fire.

Oldenberg ...{Loading}...
  1. When (the meal) is ready, in the evening and in the morning, (the wife) should say, ‘It is ready! and (the husband) with loud voice, ‘Om!’

सिद्धे सायम्प्रातर्भूतमित्युक्त ओमित्युच्चैर्ब्रूयात् १९

Oldenberg ...{Loading}...
  1. Then in a low voice: ‘May it not fail! Adoration to thee!’
