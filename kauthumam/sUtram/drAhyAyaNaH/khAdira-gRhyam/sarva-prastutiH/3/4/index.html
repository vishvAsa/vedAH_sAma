Oldenberg
  1. After having made that oblation he should recite over (the cow the verse), ‘May to thee’ (l.l. 6).

Oldenberg
  1. Let him sprinkle it with water in which barley is, with (the formula), ‘Agreeable to the Ashṭakā I sprinkle thee!’

प्रोक्ष्योल्मुकेन परिहृत्य प्रोक्षणीः पाययेत् ५

Oldenberg
  1. Having sprinkled it and carried a fire-brand round it, he should give the Prokshaṇī water (to the cow) to drink.

Oldenberg
  1. Going in a northern direction (from the fire) he should kill (the cow), the head of which is turned to the west, the feet to the north.

सञ्ज्ञप्तायां जुहुयाद्यत्पशुरिति ७

Oldenberg
  1. After it has been killed, he should sacrifice (Ājya) with (the verse), ‘If the beast’ (l.l. 8).

  1. Seizing it with one branch and with another forked branch of a sacrificially pure tree he should roast it.

  1. When it has ceased to drop, he should hew (the cow) to pieces.
