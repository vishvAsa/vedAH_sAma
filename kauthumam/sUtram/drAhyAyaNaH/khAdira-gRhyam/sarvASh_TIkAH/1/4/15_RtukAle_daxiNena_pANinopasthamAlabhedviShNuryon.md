<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

15 [^fn_999]. At the time of her courses he should touch with his right hand her secret parts with (the verse), 'May Vishṇu make thy womb ready' (MB. I, 4, 6).

[^fn_999]: 15, 16 = II, 5, 9, 10.
