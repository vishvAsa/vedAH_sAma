<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

स्विष्टकृतस्सकृदुपस्तीर्य द्विर्भृगूणां सकृद्धविषो द्विरभिघार्याग्नये स्विष्टकृते स्वाहेति प्रागुदीच्यां जुहुयात् २४
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

24. At the Svishṭakr̥t oblation he 'spreads under' once - twice if he is a Bhr̥gu - (cuts off) once (a portion) of the Havis, sprinkles (Ājya) on it twice, and sacrifices it in a north-eastern direction with (the formula), 'To Agni Svishṭakr̥t Svāhā!
</details>
