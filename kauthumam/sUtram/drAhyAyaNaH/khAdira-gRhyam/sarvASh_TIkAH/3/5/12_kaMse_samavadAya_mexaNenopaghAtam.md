<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

कंसे समवदाय मेक्षणेनोपघातं जुहुयात् स्वाहा सोमाय पितृमते स्वाहाऽग्नये कव्यवाहनायेति १२

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

12. Cutting off (the prescribed portions of the sacrificial food, and putting those portions) into the brazen vessel he should sacrifice, picking out (portions of the Havis) with the pot-ladle, with (the formulas), 'Svāhā to Soma Pitr̥mat! Svāhā to Agni Kavyavāhana!' (MB. II. 3, 1, 2.)
</details>
