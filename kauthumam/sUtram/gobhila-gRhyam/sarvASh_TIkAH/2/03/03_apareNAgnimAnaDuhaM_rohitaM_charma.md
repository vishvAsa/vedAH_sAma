<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

अपरेणाग्निमानडुहं रोहितं चर्म प्राग्ग्रीवमुत्तरलोमास्तीर्णं भवति ३
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

3. [^2]  To the west of the fire a red bull's hide has been spread out, with the neck to the east and with the hair outside.


[^2]:  This is the standing description of the bull's hide used at the Śrauta or Gṛhya ceremonies; comp. Śāṅkhāyana I, 16, 1 note.
</details>
