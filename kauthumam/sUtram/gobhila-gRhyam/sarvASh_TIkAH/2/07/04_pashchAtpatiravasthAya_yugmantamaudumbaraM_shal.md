<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

पश्चात्पतिरवस्थाय युग्मन्तमौदुम्बरं शलाटुग्रथ्नमाबध्नात्ययमूर्जावतो वृक्ष इति ४
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

4. [^3]  Her husband, standing behind her, ties (to her neck) an Udumbara branch with an even number of unripe fruits on it, with (the verse), 'Rich in sap is this tree' (MB. I, 5, 1).


[^3]:  śalāṭugrathnam should be emended, in my opinion, so as to read salāṭugrapsam. Comp. Pāraskara I, 15, 4: yugmena saṭālugrapsenaudumbareṇa. Āśvalāyana I, 14,4: yugmena śalāṭuglapsena. Hiraṇyakeśin II, 1: salātugrapsam upasaṃgṛhya.
</details>
