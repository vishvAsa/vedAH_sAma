<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

22. [^3]  'Let him avoid an Aśvattha tree on the east-side (of his house), and a Plakṣa on the south-side, a Nyagrodha on the west-side, and on the north-side an Udumbara.


[^3]:  22-24.
