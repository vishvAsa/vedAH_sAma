20. For an involuntary discharge caused by fear or sickness, or happening during sleep, and if for seven days the fire-oblations and begging have been neglected, (a student) shall make an offering of clarified [^13]  butter or (place) two pieces of fuel (in the fire) reciting the two (verses called) Retasya.


[^13]:  Manu II, 181, 187; Yājñavalkya III, 278, 281. The Retasyās are found Taittirīya Āraṇyaka I, 30.
