33. Or his usual food distinguished by a (particularly careful) preparation. [^29] 


[^29]:  'But if (the host is) not able (to afford dainties), he shall prepare that same food which is daily used in his house, distinguished in the preparation, i.e. by adding pepper and the like condiments, by frying it, and so forth.'--Haradatta.
